ABOUT US

The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Club was introduced exclusively for the ECE students to gain hands-on exposure in the field of IoT, Robotics, and Artificial Intelligence. provide guidance and learning support to our student community.
The peer mentorship program provides 120 hours of training from basic electronic devices to projects like line follower robots, and robotic Automation, thereby making students industry ready. The RAIC hopes to make a lasting impact on its members and volunteers by ushering them into a lifelong learning experience.
